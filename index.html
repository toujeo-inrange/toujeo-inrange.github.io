InRange study, presented at Advanced Technologies & Treatments for Diabetes Conference, is the first randomized controlled trial to compare second generation basal insulin analogues Toujeo (insulin glargine 300 U/mL) and insulin degludec 100 U/mL, in adults with T1DM, using Time-In-Range (TIR) as the primary endpoint.
